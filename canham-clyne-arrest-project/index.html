Arrest Data


Beginning in 2013, UMPD's jurisdiction expanded to include much of the City of College Park. For several years thereafter, arrests fell slowly, reaching a low in 2018 with 380 arrests. This year, UMPD has arrested almost 300 more people. UMPD reports this data on their website. These charts were generated with that data, by copying the arrest records for each year and analyzing them with Googlesheets.


Arrests by Year 2013-2018


In total, UMPD carried out 3796 arrests between the start of 2013 and mid-November 2019, when the data for this story was culled. A majority of those arrested are black. UMPD only records four racial categories, White, Black, American Indian/Alaskan, and Asian/Pacific Islander. Since the data doesn't include latino/hispanic, it'd difficult to tell the overall distribution of arrests.
